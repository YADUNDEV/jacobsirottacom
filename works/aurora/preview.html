Attached is a few of the audio files produced for the Josiah Lebowitz's upcoming visual novel Aurora's Nightmare. Produced between 6/29 to 7/20, I was brought on to help finish the audio work for the title. I was given 40 prompts and worked with Audacity and Mixcraft 7 to produce roughly 375 audio files (including alternate and revised sounds) in response.

Software:

Audacity, Mixcraft 7

Role:

Sound Design (Contract Work)
