Gameplay Footage

About

0.2.0 Would mark the first publically released update to the game. It would add a new stage, Failure City, which was the final level from one of my most formative games, ROBOCAT 02 II DUO / PI JUDGEMENT DAYS. This would be the first stage to feature multiple music tracks, 2 original remixes of tracks from the original game. This patch would also add customizable stock counts, new visual effects, and some character balance changes, buffing Sputnik and Carpenter Chicken.

Original Patch Notes

v. 0.2.0 Patch Notes

The final level from ROBOCAT 02 II DUO / PI JUDGEMENT DAYS, Failure City, is now a playable stage in Abomination Fight Pit Deluxe! It features various remixes of tracks from the ROBOCAT soundtrack.

New Features and Improvements

  • You can now change the stock count.
  • Fighter-unique stock icons have been added.
  • New visual effects have been added (dash, land, ground bounce, and kb angle particle effects)
  • Various ui improvements, including new (but wip) fonts.
  • When both players play the same character, the second player will be tinted slightly.
  • SDI factor increased from 20 to 27 px.
  • Reduced Intangibility frames from an air dash from 30 frames to 20.
  • Various bug fixes.

Sputnik Changes:

  • Hurtbox size decreased.
  • Horizontal air speed increased from 350 to 380.

Carpenter Chicken Changes:

  • Startup and endlag reduced on ground and air Hammer Throw
  • Startup and endlag reduced on normal up air attack.
